Hallo!
I'm currently a senior in the department of computer science and electrical engineering at KAIST, South Korea. I'm doing research under Professor Minhyuk Sung at the Geometric AI Lab. I'm broadly interested in geometry processing, machine learning, and physical simulation. More specifically, I'm interested in developing tools that enable more intuitive and creative control over 3D content. I'm always looking for collaboration opportunities, so feel free to contact me!

In my free time, I also enjoy painting, photography, and write programs!
